# Trademarks and Branding Guidelines

This document governs the use of AIMO-related marks.

## Covered Marks (non-exhaustive)
- AIMO
- AIMOaaS
- Certified AIMO
- AIMO Standard

## General Rules
1. Do not imply endorsement, certification, or affiliation without explicit permission.
2. Do not use the marks in a way that may cause confusion with the official project.
3. Do not alter official logos (if/when provided) unless explicitly permitted.

## Compatibility / Conformance Claims
- "Compatible with AIMO Standard" or "Implements AIMO Standard" may be used only if:
  1) All MUST requirements in the normative spec are met, and
  2) The official validator (or an equivalent) passes without modification of rules.

## Prohibited Uses
- Using AIMO marks as your product/company name.
- Using AIMO marks in a manner suggesting official certification where none exists.
